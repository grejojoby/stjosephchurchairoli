CELEBRATING FATHERHOOD IN CHRIST

Pithruvedi is an organization that welcomes all married men from families in the Parish to become members. The association is dedicated to St. Joseph, the Patron saint, who serves as a role model for husbands and fathers through his complete surrender to God's will, diligent protection of the holy family, and exemplary self-control.
"The righteous lead blameless lives; blessed are their children after them."
Proverbs 20:7
On October 18, 2009, Pithruvedi had its formal inauguration from His Beatitude Cardinal Mar George Alencherry, the present Major Archbishop of the Syro-Malabar Catholic Church, at the Presentation Convent School Ground in Nerul. In this first assembly, H.E. Mar Thomas Elavanal promulgated the Pithruvedi By-Laws.

The primary objective of this association is to bring the members of the parish community together in their shared religious beliefs by nurturing them with spiritual principles and teachings. Pithruvedi endeavors to instill the significance of selflessness, prioritizing family values, and leading the family in a life centered around prayer and worship.

The members of Pithruvedi solely host the feast of St. Joseph, the patron saint of the parish, which includes liturgy, choir, and Ottunercha, in addition to actively participating and carrying out each of their respective tasks throughout every parish feast and programme. Additionally, Pithruvedi takes part in diocesan events like tug-of-war contests and badminton competitions.
