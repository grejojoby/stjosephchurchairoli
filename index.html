Community Prayer,
St. Joseph, Our Patron

O Holy and Glorious St. Joseph, beloved Patron of Airoli Parish, we call upon you and your dearest Spouse, our Mother Mary, to intercede for us before your Son Jesus for the continued well being of our Parish, and to help us at all times. You were the Father of Our Lord, and with Mary, you cared for Him and provided for all the needs of the Holy Family. Hear us now, in our times of special needs and bring our requests to your Son, that He may send His continued graces upon us and bring to a happy solution the situatuin that confronts us. Dearest Mother Mary, with Joseph, we ask you to assist us and to be our advocate for the continued spiritual and physical growth and well being for our Parish.
Joseph, son of David, and husband of Mary - we honour you, Guardiann of Redeemer - we adore the child you named Jesus.
Saint Joseph, patron of the universal church, pray for us, that like you we may live totally dedicated to the interests of the Saviour. Amen.

From Vicar's Desk

Our Dear Parish Priest

A parish is a spiritual borne: a safe haven to experience Cod's love on our journey towards heaven. A parish is a community where everyone should feel welcomed. It is a place where we come to celebrate and commemorate events of our lives. Like a personal family, a parish should always be ready to help one another and share in each other's lives. It is a place of prayer, renewal, and strengthening of our faith through the reception of the Sacraments. We should feel the presence of the Holy Spirit guiding us to Jesus Christ, who is our salvation. As a spiritual home, our Parish cannot accomplish everything, but we can cooperate with the Master in building a safe environment where all can feel welcomed, included, and accepted. Our parish has been blessed with so many active parishioners who enjoy generously giving of themselves in service to fellow parishioners and members of the greater community. Our Baptismal calling encourages us to be active members of the Mystical Body of Christ, sharing our gifts with others to continue building the Kingdom of God. May the Good Lord continue to bless you in abundance and may He send the Holy Spirit to touch the hearts of others to join us in our unity to do all that we do for the greater glory of God. A parish directory which usually presents, photos of the members of a parish, together with photos of different groups and committees is a wonderful way to recognize each other and better view ourselves as a community It also a visible opportunity for a new parish priest to put a face to the names of his new parishioners. I sincerely thank the Trustees, Parish Council Members and Directory Committee for their hard workand cooperation. It is my prayer that the parish flourishes in faith and love for our Lord and one another, for many years to come. Let us always remember the words of St Paul the Apostle: "So we, who are many, are one body in Christ, and individuallywe are members one of another" (Rom.12:5). In Jesus Christ

- Fr. Lijo Velliyamkandathil

Parish Priest
